About us

Meet The Owner

Hello! My name is Loreto Araya, and I am a chef. Since I can remember, I have been always been surrounded by gastronomy. When I moved to New Zealand I began learning about vegan food and I fell in love with it - the idea of having a everyday diet of only vegan food was amazing. I created Karmic Kitchen to make vegan options more accessible for the people in the markets.

Our Vision

Karmic Kitchen was born from my dream of being able to create a living doing something I believe in and love. I am always trying to find the best products for my food and doing my best to support small businesses in my community. My goal is to use Karmic Kitchen to make vegan options more accessible for the people and to show everyone that they can find something tasty out there.
